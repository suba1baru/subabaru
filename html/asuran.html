SPOTSTAR-DHANUSH MOVIES

ASURAN

In the late 1970s, in Kovilpatti, a man and a boy are trekking across a river, carrying homemade bombs. In another part of the village, a woman, a man, and a girl are also hiding from the police. Past: The narrator reveals that the man is Sivasaami and the boy is his son Chidambaram.
