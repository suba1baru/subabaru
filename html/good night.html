COMEDY/ACTION/DRAMA

GOOD NIGHT

The film stars Manikandan, alongside Meetha Raghunath, Ramesh Thilak, Raichal Rabecca, Balaji Sakthivel and Bagavathi Perumal. It follows Mohan, an IT youngster, who has a snoring problem, which affects his life. The film was released on 12 May 2023, and received positive reviews from critics.
