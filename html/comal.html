COMEDY/ACTION/DRAMA

COMALI

The film stars Jayam Ravi, Kajal Aggarwal, Yogi Babu, Samyuktha Hegde and K. S. Ravikumar. It revolves around a man who has missed 16 years of his life due to being in a coma, and struggles to adapt to the new improved world. Comali was released on 15 August 2019 coinciding with Indian Independence Day.
