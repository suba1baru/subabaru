Sivakarthikeyan (born 17 February 1985[1]), also known by the initialism SK, is an Indian actor, playback singer, lyricist, film producer and television presenter, who worked predominantly in Tamil films. He was a recipient of a Tamil Nadu State Film Award, four Edison Awards, four SIIMA Awards, and two other Vijay Awards.
