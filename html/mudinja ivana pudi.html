MUDINJA IVANA PUDI

Mudinja Ivana Pudi is a 2016 Tamil-language drama and suspense film about a real estate agent who also works as a burglar and is pursued by a corrupt police officer. The film stars Kannada superstar Sudeep as Shivam, the burglar, and also features Nithya Menen, Sai Ravi, Prakash Raj, and others.
